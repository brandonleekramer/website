Brandon Kramer, Ph.D.

Email - GitHub

Hi, my name is Brandon Kramer and I’m a User Experience (UX) Researcher at Edge & Node and The Graph protocol. While I was formally trained as a sociologist, I used my background to advance decentralized technologies in the blockchain industry. During my academic career, I incorporated aspects of data science, science & technology studies, philosophy, economics, and design to study innovation and ethics in science, tech, and health. As my publications and projects pages detail, my main focus during graduate school was the conceptualization of sex/gender, race/ethnicity, and diversity in biomedical research. Later, as a postdoc at the University of Virginia, I worked on a team that studied the scope and impact of open-source software, which materialized into an interest in the blockchain industry. At Edge & Node, I now work on the design team working to improve the usability of The Graph - an open-source API designed to efficiently query, index, and store data from across multiple blockchains in a decentralized manner. Over the course of my career, I have acquired a breadth of methodological skills, including survey design and dissemination, big data analytics, software development, social network analysis, natural language processing, and data visualization. I obtained a Ph.D. in Sociology from Rutgers University in 2019 and previously held research positions at the University of Iowa, Rutgers University, Barnard College, and the University of Virginia.
